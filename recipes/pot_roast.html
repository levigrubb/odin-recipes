Slow-Cooked Pot-Roast

Required ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme (or 1 teaspoon dried thyme)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (or 1 teaspoon dried rosemary)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4-6 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 4-6 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven:Prehead your oven to 325oF (160oC).
  2. Prepare the beef:Pat the beef roast dry with paper towels. Season it generously with salt and pepper on all sides.
  3. Sear the beef: Heat the olive oil in a large oven-safe pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the beef roast and sear it until browned on all sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove the beef from the pot and set it aside.
  4. Cook the aromatics: In the same pot, add the chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped carrots, and chopped celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Deglaze the pot: Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), scraping the bottom of the pot to loosen any browned bits. Stir in the tomato paste until well combined.
  6. Add herbs and beef: Tie the thyme, rosemary, and bay leaves together with kitchen twine to make a bouquet garni. Place the bouquet garni and the seared beef roast back into the pot.
  7. Slow cook: Cover the pot with a lid and transfer it to the preheated oven. Allow the roast to cook for about 3-4 hours, or until the beef is fork-tender and falls apart easily.
  8. Add vegetables: About 1 hour before the roast is done, add the potato and carrot chunks to the pot. Continue cooking until the vegetables are tender and the beef is fully cooked.
  9. Serve: Once the roast is onde, remove it from the pot and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve the pot roast slices with the vegetables and some of the cooking liquid as gravy.
  10. Enjoy: Enjoy your delicious slow-cooked pot roast with your favorite sides!

This recipe yields a tender and flavorful pot roast that's perfect for a comforting meal, especially on a chilly day. Adjust the seasonings and vegetables to suit your taste preferences, and feel free to customize the recipe as you like!
